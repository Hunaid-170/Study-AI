Frequently asked questions

Is StudyAI free to use?
Yes — the Free plan gives you 10 AI doubt solves per day, basic quizzes, and a single-subject planner with no time limit. Upgrade to Student Pro anytime for unlimited access.
Which exams does StudyAI support?
StudyAI supports school curricula (CBSE, ICSE, and state boards), competitive exams like JEE, NEET, UPSC, and GATE, plus international tests including SAT, ACT, IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, and GMAT.
Can I upload handwritten notes?
Yes. Snap a photo of handwritten or printed notes and StudyAI will read, organize, and summarize the content — including diagrams and equations.
Is my data private and secure?
Your notes and conversations are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell student data, and institutions can request dedicated data-handling agreements.
Can my school or coaching center get a bulk plan?
Absolutely. The Institution plan includes bulk licensing, an admin dashboard for tracking student progress, and curriculum alignment. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.
